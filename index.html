Statement of Work — Single-Pass Human-Dependency Review

(No Cost · One-Time · Non-Iterative)

This is a structural review, not a position statement.

I’m offering a one-time, no-cost, single-pass structural review of an existing AI system, feature, or automated workflow.

This is not consulting, not advisory, not evaluation, and not a proposal for future work.

The purpose is narrow and explicit:

To document where a system continues operating, closing, or resolving outcomes in the absence of an explicitly designated, accountable human decision-holder.

Scope (Strictly Limited)

Included

  • One (1) existing AI system, feature, or automated flow
  • One (1) execution only (no iteration, no follow-up)

The review examines:

  • Decision paths and closure points
  • Declared human roles vs. actual enforcement
  • Timeouts, thresholds, fallbacks, or auto-resolution behavior
  • What occurs under documented absence or delayed human response conditions

Beaxis evaluates continuity, not correctness.

Explicit Exclusions

This review does not:

  • Recommend changes
  • Provide opinions, guidance, or validation
  • Evaluate ethics, alignment, UX, performance, or outcomes
  • Participate in implementation, governance, or decision-making
  • Continue after delivery

There is no second phase.

Inputs Required (Written Only)

  • A simple flow diagram or written flow description
  • Declared human roles
  • Closure rules, timeouts, escalation logic
  • Read-only documentation access (if applicable)

No interviews. No intent. No justification.

Deliverables

  1. Continuity Map — Identifying where the system advances with or without human presence.
  2. Human-Dependency Report — Documenting observed behavior under documented absence or delayed response conditions.
  3. Decision Responsibility Mapping — Identifying where no enforced human decision responsibility exists.

Deliverables describe what is, not what should be done.

Boundaries & Protections

  • No interpretation provided
  • No assurance, certification, or endorsement of system behavior
  • No attribution or public reference
  • No reuse of materials
  • No obligation on either side beyond delivery
  • Full responsibility for any use of the materials remains with the client

Cost

  • $0
  • One-time
  • Single execution
  • No extensions

Closing Condition

This engagement ends upon delivery of the documents.

No pitch will follow. No outreach will continue unless explicitly requested.

Beaxis exists to make visible where systems operate without a human at the wheel — nothing more.
